Research in
Natural Language Interfaces

I am interested in different aspects of Human Computer Interaction (HCI) related to Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Generation (NLG).

I am currently working on applying these concepts to the Open Government Data and Government Transparency

My past work has focussed on related problematics such as: Persuasive Dialogue (Ph.D. Thesis), Topic Extraction, Grammar Parsing, Evaluation Methodologies.

Current Projects

I have joined the LIDET at the IME - University of São Paulo as a Reasearch Fellow to work on a project to improve the participation of citizens in the parliaments of Brasil.

In the past, I have been working with the KnowDive group at the University of Trento (Italy) within these projects: Live Memories, INSEMTIVES, Glocal.
